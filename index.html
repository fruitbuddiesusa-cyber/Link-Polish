Frequently Asked Questions

Is Link Polish really free?
Yes! Link Polish is 100% free to use for core formatting features including bold, italic, underline, strikethrough, bullet lists, and more. The Pro version unlocks additional features like copy-to-clipboard, custom signatures, and viral hooks for a one-time $9 payment.
How does the text formatting work?
Link Polish uses Unicode characters to create formatted text. When you click Bold, Italic, or other formatting buttons, we convert your regular text into special Unicode characters that appear bold, italic, etc. These work on LinkedIn, Twitter, and most other platforms because they're actual characters, not HTML or markdown.
Will my formatted text work on LinkedIn?
Yes! Unicode formatting works on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, and most other platforms. Since the formatting uses actual Unicode characters rather than HTML, your formatted text will appear correctly almost everywhere.
What's included in the Pro version?
Pro unlocks: Templates, Viral Hooks, Find & Replace, Special Characters, Custom Signatures, and priority access to new features. Copy to Clipboard is FREE for everyone! It's a one-time $9 payment for lifetime access — no subscriptions.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. All formatting happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server. We don't collect, store, or have access to any of your content. Your privacy is 100% guaranteed.
Can I use this on mobile?
Yes! Link Polish is fully responsive and works on mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to your screen size for the best experience.
Do I need to create an account?
No! There's no signup required. Just open the tool and start formatting. Your preferences and Pro status are saved in your browser's local storage.
What browsers are supported?
Link Polish works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version for the best experience.
