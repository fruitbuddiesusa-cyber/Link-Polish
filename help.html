Text Cleaner

Remove whitespace & invisible chars

Paste text from other sources and it often comes with extra spaces, tabs, trailing whitespace, or invisible zero-width characters. The Text Cleaner strips all of that out in one click, leaving you with clean, LinkedIn-ready content.

What it removes:

  • ✓ Multiple consecutive spaces → single space
  • ✓ Trailing whitespace on each line
  • ✓ Zero-width characters (U+200B, U+FEFF, etc.)
  • ✓ Non-breaking spaces → regular spaces
  • ✓ Multiple blank lines → single blank line

🎯 Tips for Great LinkedIn Posts

Start with a hook. The first 2 lines are all people see before clicking "see more." Make them count.

Use white space. Short paragraphs and double line breaks make your post scannable on mobile.

End with a CTA. Ask a question, invite opinions, or encourage sharing to boost engagement.

Use emojis sparingly. 3–5 emojis per post is the sweet spot. Too many looks unprofessional.

Format key phrases. Use bold for key takeaways and italic for emphasis — but don't overdo it.

Keep it under 1,500 chars. Shorter posts tend to get more engagement than long-form content.
